About Dhanyanetra

Dhanyanetra is an AI-powered agricultural intelligence platform designed to detect famine risk early and help farmers, researchers, and government agencies make data-driven decisions.

Using real-time climate data, satellite imagery, and socio-economic analysis, we provide early warnings to prevent crop failures and food crises.
